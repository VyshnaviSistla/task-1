Personal life

Vijay married Sangeetha Sornalingam,[151][152] a Sri Lankan Tamil whom he met in the United Kingdom, on 25 August 1999.[153][154] They have two children.[155] Vijay's son made a cameo appearance with his father in Vettaikaaran (2009) and his daughter portrayed a small role as her father's pre-teen daughter in Theri (2016). On 5 February 2020, the Income Tax Department raided Vijay's residence in Chennai and inquired about potential tax evasion, making note of his investment in immovable properties, which he inherited from the production studio AGS Entertainment.[156] It was reported that Vijay and AGS Entertainment producer Anbu Cheliyan were suspected of undisclosed payments and alleged tax fraud. Nearly ₹65 crore was seized by the officials from Cheliyan's residence.[157] The investigation took place while Vijay was shooting for his film Master in Cuddalore.[158] On 12 March, officials stated that nothing significant was found during the raid.[159] Opponents of the Bharatiya Janata Party accused them of politically targeting Vijay through such raids because he was critical of the them on demonetization and Goods and Services Tax (India) in the film Mersal.[160] On 13 July 2021, the Madras High Court dismissed a writ petition filed by Vijay in 2012 seeking exemption of the Entry Tax for his Rolls-Royce Ghost car that was imported from England. It imposed a fine of ₹1 lakh which was allotted to the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Public COVID relief fund. Justice S.M. Subramaniam said that Vijay's fan base considers him as a hero and he was expected to be one instead of a "reel" hero, further calling it an anti-national habit.[161] On 15 July 2021, Vijay filed an appeal against defamatory statements made by the judge against him in Madras High court.[162] On 20 July 2021, Vijay's appeal against the tax exemption case issue and defamatory statements was moved to a different tax bench sector of the court.[163] On 27 July 2021, a two-judge bench of Madras High court stayed the earlier passed order by Judge S.M. Subramaniam that included the critical remarks and also stayed the order of a ₹1 lakh fine amount.[164] On 25 January 2022, the court dismissed and removed the defamatory critical statements made by Judge S.M. Subramaniam against Vijay.[165][166] On 15 July 2022, the court declared that no fine should be imposed on the car imported by since he had paid the full entry tax before January 2019, closing out the case in the process.[167][168][169] Wealth As of 2021, Vijay's net worth is ₹4200 million or ₹420 crore.[170] Vijay earned a ₹100 crore salary for Beast,[171] and also earned an estimated ₹120 crore—₹150 crore for Varisu, marking him among the highest paid Indian actors.[7][172][173] In 2023, he earned ₹200 crore salary and became one of the top paid actor in the world.[14] Artistry and honours Main article: List of awards and nominations received by Vijay Vijay receiving his first stamp album from Indian Prime Minister Manmohan Singh in 2006 Vijay's wax figures were unveiled in several parts of India, especially in Kanyakumari Museum.[174][175] The Arabic Kuthu music dance video from the film Beast garnered 20 million views in 1 day.[176][177][178] The song "Ranjithame" from Varisu was well received in Pakistan and Bangladesh.[179] Vijay's official Instagram page was the third fastest to reach 1 million followers in the world.[180] News 18 describes Vijay as a "versatile actor" and a "fantastic playback singer, dancer and philanthropist".[181][182] Odisha TV reported that "While fans are crazy for [Vijay's] movies, he has a set of dedicated audience who enjoy his dance moves".[183] Zee media reported that Vijay amazed audiences with his acting and screen presence.[184] The Times of India labelled Vijay's dance moves in his film songs as "energetic and effortless".[185] Vijay received an honorary doctorate from the Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute in 2007 in honour of his achievements in the film industry.[186] Vijay was awarded the 'Best International Actor of 2018' in the United Kingdom for Mersal.[187]
